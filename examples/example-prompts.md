# Example Prompts for KartSan

Try these with your KartSan skill installed. Upload the `sample_telemetry.csv` file when prompted.

---

## Session analysis (English)

> Here's my telemetry from today's practice. How did I do? Where am I losing time?

*Upload `sample_telemetry.csv` with this prompt. KartSan will produce an interactive dashboard with charts, KPI cards, sector analysis, and coaching advice.*

---

## Session analysis (Portuguese)

> Aqui está a minha telemetria do treino de hoje. Como correu? Onde estou a perder tempo?

*Same file, Portuguese response. KartSan detects language automatically.*

---

## Setup advice

> It rained overnight but the track is drying. We're running MG Reds on a Mini Swift. What tire pressures should we start with, and how should we adjust gearing?

---

## Racecraft question

> I keep getting passed on the outside into Turn 3. It's a medium-speed right-hander at the end of a short straight. Can you explain to me what I should do?

---

## Concept explanation for young driver

> Can you explain to my 8-year-old what the racing line is? Maybe with a drawing?

*KartSan will create an interactive HTML file with SVG diagrams.*

---

## Rotax rules question

> Is it legal to change the exhaust restrictor on a Micro MAX? What about changing the main jet?

---

## MyChron setup help

> I just got a MyChron 6 2T. How should I set it up for Micro MAX? What display pages do you recommend?

---

## Track-specific advice

> We're racing at Zuera this weekend. Any tips on gearing and setup for that track?

---

## Pre-race pump-up

> We have the final race in 20 minutes. I'm nervous. What should I focus on?

---

## Multi-session comparison

> Here's telemetry from last weekend and this weekend. Am I improving?

*Upload two CSV files for comparison analysis.*
